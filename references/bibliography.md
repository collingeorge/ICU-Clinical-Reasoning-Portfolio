# CTICU Core Bibliography  
**Clinically Curated, Attending-Level Evidence Base**  
Version 1.1 — December 2025

This bibliography highlights the essential studies, guidelines, and high-impact papers that inform modern critical care, cardiothoracic intensive care, mechanical ventilation, shock management, and ECMO physiology.  
Entries are grouped by domain for rapid reference during rounds, research preparation, and case review.

---

# 1. Hemodynamics, Shock, & Perfusion

### Foundational Physiology
1. Guyton AC. "Circulatory Physiology: Cardiac Output and Its Regulation."

### Shock Classification & Management
3. Jentzer J et al. "Shock Classification in the ICU: Physiology and Clinical Application." Mayo Clin Proc.

7. Nguyen HB et al. "ScvO₂-Guided Therapy." Crit Care Med.

# Key Takeaways

- These references represent the **evidence foundation of modern critical care and CTICU practice**.  
- They should be used for cross-checking clinical decisions, improving case write-ups, and supporting attending-level reasoning.  
- The portfolio intentionally includes **classic physiology, landmark trials, and recent high-impact literature** to ensure clinical reasoning is both timeless and up to date.
